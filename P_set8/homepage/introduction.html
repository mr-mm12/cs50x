Top 10 Cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin (BTC)

Created in 2008 by Satoshi Nakamoto. Country origin: Unknown / Global.

Ethereum (ETH)

Launched in 2015 by Vitalik Buterin. Country origin: Canada/Russia.

Tron (TRX)

Founded in 2017 by Justin Sun. Country origin: China.

Litecoin (LTC)

Created in 2011 by Charlie Lee. Country origin: USA.

Ripple (XRP)

Founded in 2012 by Chris Larsen & Jed McCaleb. Country origin: USA.

Cardano (ADA)

Created in 2017 by Charles Hoskinson. Country origin: USA/Japan.

Polkadot (DOT)

Launched in 2020 by Gavin Wood. Country origin: UK.

Solana (SOL)

Founded in 2020 by Anatoly Yakovenko. Country origin: USA.

Dogecoin (DOGE)

Created in 2013 by Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer. Country origin: USA.

Chainlink (LINK)

Launched in 2017 by Sergey Nazarov. Country origin: USA.
